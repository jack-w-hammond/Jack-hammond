Soaked Film

Red Wine soaked in 35mm Camera Film

The Hidden Mysteries is a digital magazine at the centre of a community of bold young adults and teenagers who have an insatiable curiosity. We explore the secrets of science and nature, going to the places you can't. We travel to the strangest landscapes on earth and meet the most elusive human beings, telling their stories through immersive visual journalism.

For the third Issue of our series The Hidden Mysteries, we have decided to take the reader on a different kind of journey. The Hidden Mysteries Vol.3 explores how soaking 35mm camera film in different household chemicals and carcinogens can give strange and random effects on photos, whilst learning how you can achieve the same results and create unique images for yourself. This series is supposed to look like a ‘cookbook’ for experimental photography.
